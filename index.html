Top three activities to do at Stockholm

An apocalyptic underground art tour

Stockholm’s metro network is often dubbed the world’s longest art gallery for a good reason; most stations have art in some form or other.

Djurgården’s world-class museums

Stockholmers are obsessed with nature, and nowhere is better than Djurgården’s tranquil waterside and woodland trails.

Sail through the 30,000-island archipelago

Stockholm’s city centre consists of 14 islands, but that’s nothing compared with the tens of thousands that make up its sprawling archipelago.
